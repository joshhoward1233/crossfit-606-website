ABOUT US

Detailing some of the accomplishments of our coaches here at CrossFit 606

All of our coaches are experienced and certified with CrossFit, which will ensure you get the best experience while at our gym!

Coach Shea Moody

Here we go Tri-County! I was born and raised in Harlan, KY. I graduated from Harlan High School in 2005 and then played football for a year at The University of Cumberland. In 2009, I decided to join the United States Marine Corps and I truly believe it was the best decision I've ever made. During my time in the Marine Corps, I found CrossFit and it changed my life! After getting out of the Marine Corps in 2013, I decided to stay in Southern California, go back to school and coach CrossFit. This began my true journey on the love on changing peoples' lives. A year passed and the opportunity came to move to Seattle, WA and continue to coach, so I took it. Living in Seattle for two years, I coached at three different CrossFit gyms and had guidance from three of the best gym owners the community has to offer. After thinking long and hard, I decided to come back to Kentucky and pursue my dream of opening my own gym. Now that dream has become a reality! I'm looking forward to being a part of the community while brining CrossFit and a healthy lifestyle to the Tri-County area. Together we can improve our health and fitness!
